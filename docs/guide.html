Mortals & Gods - Build Your Own Pantheon!

Found a religion, raise altars, and earn boons from gods you design.

NeoForge 1.21.1  |  modid mortalsgods  |  v1.0.1  |  by Barunn (NewtL)  |  soft-deps: Pehkui, Iron's Spells, War 'n Nobility

Live on CurseForge

Mortals & Gods is a data-driven worship mod. Stack ore beneath an enchanting table to raise an Altar, wall it into a Sanctum, and open a full-screen worship UI to venerate a pantheon you control. Each opposed pair forces a choice, life or death, craft or magic, order or slaughter, and you may follow only one god of a pair at a time. Offer to your chosen gods and their boons grow along a saturating devotion curve, then fade if you neglect the faith. Mortals can ascend too: a living ruler (the Emperor) can be worshipped or hunted, his body swelling or shrinking with the realm's favor.

Nothing to craft, nothing to find. Out of the box the mod ships the Warborn Realms pantheon, art, and lore, so you can just play. You only touch the config folder if you want to build your own gods; a mistyped god is skipped with a log warning while the rest keep working.

The Altar

There are no crafted items. Worship happens at an Altar you build: an enchanting table standing on a 3x3 platform of ore blocks, enclosed inside a Sanctum.

O O O O O O O O O

Enchanting Table sits centered on top  •  O = any counted ore block (Copper, Iron, Gold, Emerald, Diamond, Netherite)

  • Arming: all nine blocks under the table must be counted ore blocks, and the table must sit inside an enclosed room (the Sanctum). The Sanctum is detected by a flood-fill of open space; if it leaks outdoors (larger than 5000 air cells) it counts as open air and does not arm.
  • Altar tier (1 to 4) is an offering multiplier, not a structure requirement. It is the total value of ore blocks adorning the temple: the 3x3 base plus every ore block bordering the Sanctum's open space (walls, floor, ceiling, pillars), each counted once. A richer, more golden temple worships harder.

Mechanics

Choosing a god

Gods sit in groups (opposed pairs) on bookmarked tabs. Within a group you may follow only one god at a time. Switching to the other god in a pair burns your old devotion to it (the opposites are jealous) and clears any charge pool tied to the abandoned god.

Offering and devotion

An offering at the Altar consumes the chosen god's currency, advances devotion by one step, and stamps a cooldown. The Altar tier is a multiplier: a Tier 3 altar consumes three times the currency but grants three devotion per offering. Currencies are item (hand over a stack), xp (spend levels), or kill (earned in combat against the rival faction, not offered at the altar).

Boons

Every boon grows on the same saturating curve:

strength = cap * (1 - e^(-devotion / tau))

cap = the ceiling  •  tau = grindiness (~63% of cap at devotion = tau, ~95% at 3 * tau)

Boon typeWhat cap means
max_healthBonus max health (40 = +20 hearts)
auto_reviveMax stored totem-style revives (charge pool, refilled by offering)
durabilityBonus fraction of max durability on crafted gear (1.0 = +100%)
reachBonus reach; needs params.attribute (block or entity interaction range)
manaBonus mana, via the Iron's Spells bridge
war_statMax potion-effect amplifier; needs params.effect (e.g. strength, resistance)
favor_theftFraction of a victim's devotion stolen per kill (Demi-God war)
pehkui_scaleMax Pehkui scale bonus; needs params.stat
attributeGeneric: params.attribute = any attribute id, cap = bonus amount
scoreboardWrites the current curve value into params.objective each second (auto-created, zeroed when dropped) for functions or KubeJS

Favor decay

Neglect a god past the grace period and its devotion bleeds off, processed per whole in-game day. War and Demi-God groups decay slower because their kill-currency is scarce.

Demi-Gods: the living Emperor

The Demi-Gods tab worships a living ruler. Net favor (loyalists' worship minus rejecters' devotion, across all players) scales the crowned player's body through Pehkui: hunt his loyalists hard enough and he shrinks until killable. The first god in a demigod group is the support side, the rest are reject sides. Power spreads evenly across Health, Strength, Speed, and Reach, each capped by config. Crown a player with /mortalsgods emperor set or list them in demigodPlayers.

Data-driven pantheon

The gods (names, lore, colors, currencies, boons, art) live in config/mortalsgods/pantheon.json, regenerated with the Warborn Realms defaults on first run alongside a README, an images/ folder, and an optional lore.txt.

  • Custom tabs: any tab id becomes a bookmark; a top-level tabs array sets order, labels, and gating. Lore is always last.
  • Exclusive tabs ("exclusive": true): follow several gods within one exclusive tab, but not a second exclusive tab at once. /mortalsgods renounce releases those pledges to switch.
  • Access gates ("require": { ... }): gate a tab, group, or god behind scoreboard tags and objective scores; failed entries are hidden server-side and cannot be worshipped by a modified client.
  • Custom pictures: drop a 200x380 PNG into config/mortalsgods/images/ named after a god's id, or point a god's art field at a filename. Read per-client.
  • Custom lore: write config/mortalsgods/lore.txt (# lines are headings, others are paragraphs).

Compatibility

All integrations are optional soft-deps, guarded at runtime; none are bundled or required.

  • Pehkui — scales the living Emperor's body (and the pehkui_scale boon). Absent = Demi-God scaling no-ops.
  • Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks — backs the mana boon.
  • War 'n Nobility — reserved hook for the live Emperor UUID (currently manual via command).

Building

./gradlew build

Output jar in build/libs/ (e.g. mortalsgods-neoforge-1.0.1.jar). Requires JDK 21. Optional integration APIs (Pehkui, Iron's Spells, War 'n Nobility) are not bundled; drop their jars into libs/ to compile against the real APIs, otherwise the bridges fall back to reflection or no-ops.
